ZILLA  BOT  GAMMA

Zilla Bot Gamma is the third version of Zilla Bot and is the latest and most extensive update of all time. The previous version, Zilla Bot Beta, had custom commands, built-in commands, responses to various emotes and texts, but was more or less a "fun bot" to simply mess around with. All that changed with the introduction of Zilla Bot Gamma. This bot retains the custom commands, while adding a whopping ton of new features including custom reactions, moderator and logging functionality, Clash of Clans API support, Discord Music bot functionality, as well as a personalized website for it, which you are reading right now.
